01 — About

Built
Different

This is not a fitness holiday. The Hybrid Weekender brings together two communities — Team Dios and The Project — for a curated 3-day experience built for hybrid athletes.

Expect a 10KM trail run, a Hyrox-style team workout with ergs and weights, a wadi hike, beach volleyball, kayaking — and two premium BBQ dinners to bring it all together.

Two communities. One weekend. Leave fitter, fuelled, and with a crew you didn't have before.

Led by Jack Graham and Luka Mihailovic — two of the most experienced coaches in the region. Every session is programmed and delivered at a high level. You're in good hands.
